Academic Programs

Bachelor of Science (BSc)

4-year undergraduate program covering fundamentals of computer science, programming, algorithms, and software engineering.

  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Software Engineering
  • Database Systems
  • Web Development
Master of Science (MSc)

2-year graduate program focusing on advanced topics in computer science and research methodologies.

  • Machine Learning
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Computer Vision
  • Research Thesis
Doctor of Philosophy (PhD)

Research-focused doctoral program for advanced study in specialized areas of computer science.

  • Original Research
  • Publication Requirements
  • Teaching Experience
  • Dissertation Defense
